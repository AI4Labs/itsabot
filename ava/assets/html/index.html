Who is Ava?

Ava is an automated personal assistant like Siri.

Unlike Siri, anyone can add functionality to Ava, like picking up your laundry, getting you coffee and scheduling meetings. But Ava goes beyond that.

Like a real person, she'll let you know when she tries a hip new restaurant, hears a funny joke, or finds an album she knows you'll love. She's always looking for something great to share with you.

Ava strives to be more than a robot. She strives to be human.

And she's always getting smarter.
