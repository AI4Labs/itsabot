Who is Ava?

Ava is an automated personal assistant like Siri.

Unlike Siri, anyone can build packages on top of Ava to add functionality, like picking up your laundry, getting you coffee and scheduling meetings. But Ava goes beyond that.

Like a real person, she'll let you know when she finds a hip new restaurant, hears a great joke, or finds an album she knows you'll love. She's always looking for things you'll like.

Ava strives to be more than a robot. She strives to be human.

And she's always getting smarter.
